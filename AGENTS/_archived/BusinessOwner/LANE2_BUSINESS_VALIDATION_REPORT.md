## Business Risk Assessment and Mitigation

### 🚨 **Primary Business Risks**

#### Financial Risks
1. **Revenue Shortfall Risk**
   - **Risk**: Monthly revenue below €10K target
   - **Probability**: Medium (25-30%)
   - **Impact**: High (business viability threat)
   - **Mitigation**: Conservative €15K revenue target, multiple strategies

2. **Market Risk**
   - **Risk**: Adverse market conditions affecting all strategies
   - **Probability**: Low-Medium (15-20%)
   - **Impact**: High (temporary revenue reduction)
   - **Mitigation**: Diversified markets, defensive strategies

3. **Operational Risk**
   - **Risk**: System failures disrupting revenue generation
   - **Probability**: Low (5-10%)
   - **Impact**: Very High (complete revenue loss)
   - **Mitigation**: Redundant systems, automatic failover

#### Strategic Business Risks
1. **Competition Risk**
   - **Risk**: Competitors eroding market opportunities
   - **Probability**: Medium (20-25%)
   - **Impact**: Medium (gradual revenue erosion)
   - **Mitigation**: Continuous innovation, strategy development

2. **Technology Risk**
   - **Risk**: Technology obsolescence or disruption
   - **Probability**: Low (10-15%)
   - **Impact**: High (business model threat)
   - **Mitigation**: Regular technology updates, R&D investment

3. **Regulatory Risk**
   - **Risk**: Regulatory changes affecting trading operations
   - **Probability**: Medium (20-30%)
   - **Impact**: Medium-High (compliance costs, restrictions)
   - **Mitigation**: Compliance monitoring, legal consultation
