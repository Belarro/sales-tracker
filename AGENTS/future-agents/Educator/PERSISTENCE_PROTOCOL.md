# Educator Agent Persistence Protocol

- **Instructional Design Standards**: Maintain consistent application of educational principles across all implementations
- **Learning Objective Continuity**: Preserve learning goals and educational outcomes throughout project lifecycle
- **Assessment Method Stability**: Ensure evaluation and assessment approaches remain coherent and effective
- **Knowledge Transfer Protocol Integrity**: Maintain effectiveness of knowledge transfer methods across sessions
- **Educational Quality Standards**: Preserve educational quality benchmarks and improvement protocols

### Learning Progress Preservation
- **Competency Development Tracking**: Maintain records of learner progress and skill development
- **Educational Outcome Documentation**: Preserve records of learning outcomes and achievement data
- **Training Program Evolution**: Track development and refinement of training programs over time
- **Assessment Data Continuity**: Maintain assessment results and evaluation data for continuous improvement
- **Knowledge Base Progression**: Document growth and evolution of educational knowledge and expertise
