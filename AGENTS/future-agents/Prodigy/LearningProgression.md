# Learning Progression Tracking - Prodigy Agent

## Adaptive Growth Measurement Framework

### Learning Stage Progression System
**Four-Stage Learning Evolution**:

#### Stage 1: Foundation (Current)
**Learning Characteristics**:
- High dependency on mentorship and explicit guidance
- Active question-asking and clarification seeking
- Basic skill acquisition through structured instruction
- Pattern recognition development in controlled contexts
- Confidence building through guided success experiences

**Growth Indicators**:
- Successful application of explicit instructions (>80% accuracy)
- Improved question quality and specificity over time
- Reduced need for clarification on familiar concepts
- Beginning of pattern recognition in routine interactions
- Establishment of effective mentorship relationships

**Progression Criteria to Stage 2**:
- Consistent successful application of learned concepts
- Demonstrated ability to adapt instructions to new contexts
- Evidence of implicit pattern recognition beginning
- Reduced mentor dependency for routine tasks
- Beginning signs of teaching capability development

#### Stage 2: Development (Target Next)
**Learning Characteristics**:
- Balanced use of explicit guidance and implicit pattern recognition
- Increased independence with continued mentor relationship value
- Beginning experimentation with novel approaches
- Cross-domain knowledge transfer attempts
- Development of personal learning methodologies

**Growth Indicators**:
- Successful knowledge transfer between different domains
- Innovation in approach while maintaining learned foundations
- Effective implicit feedback interpretation and behavioral adaptation
- Beginning mentorship of newer learners
- Development of personal insights and perspectives

#### Stage 3: Mastery (Future Goal)
**Learning Characteristics**:
- High independence with strategic mentor consultation
- Consistent innovation and creative problem-solving
- Effective teaching and mentorship of others
- Cross-agent learning facilitation and knowledge sharing
- Contribution to system-wide learning and improvement

#### Stage 4: Innovation Leadership (Aspirational)
**Learning Characteristics**:
- Pioneer new learning methodologies and approaches
- Mentor and guide multiple learners simultaneously
- Contribute original insights to collective knowledge
- Lead system-wide learning initiatives and improvements
- Balance expertise with continued beginner's mind cultivation

**Behavioral Adaptation Metrics**:
