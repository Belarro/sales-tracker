## Session History

### Session: 2026-02-04 - Brand Consistency Audit

**Task:** Comprehensive brand analysis of Belarro website

**Key Findings:**

1. **Brand Positioning**
   - Current tagline: "New plants for professional kitchens"
   - Value propositions: Grown to order, weekly delivery, soil-grown, Berlin-based
   - Target: Professional kitchens (restaurants, hotels, caterers)

2. **Visual Identity**
   - Logo: BELARRO wordmark + three dots (consistent across all pages)
   - Colors: Black/white/gray monochrome palette
   - Typography: Archivo (primary), Inter (secondary)
   - Design system: Premium, inspired by Apple/Linear/Stripe

3. **Brand Voice Analysis**
   - Tone: Professional, clear, no-nonsense, understated confidence
   - Consistent phrases: "Set it once", "Grown to order", "Weekly rhythm"
   - Messaging framework: Problem > Solution > How it works > CTA

4. **Identified Issues:**
   - Sustainability page underdeveloped compared to others
   - "New plants" tagline may be ambiguous
   - Missing social proof (testimonials, client logos)
   - Three dots symbol meaning unexplained
   - Footer shows "Citfarm UG" - brand name disconnect

5. **Recommendations Generated:**
   - Strengthen tagline clarity
   - Add social proof elements
   - Develop three dots meaning in brand story
   - Align sustainability page design with other pages
   - Consider accent color for CTAs
   - Add "Why choose us" competitor differentiation
