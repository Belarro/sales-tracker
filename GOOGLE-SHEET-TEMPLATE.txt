====================================================
IMPORTANT NOTES
====================================================

1. The headers MUST be separated by TAB characters (not spaces)
2. They MUST be in row 1 (the first row)
3. Spelling and capitalization MUST match exactly
4. Don't add extra columns
5. Don't change the order of columns

====================================================
HOW TO PASTE CORRECTLY
====================================================

1. Copy the entire header line above
2. Click on cell A1 in your Google Sheet
3. Paste (Ctrl+V or Cmd+V)
4. The headers should spread across columns A through O

If they all paste into one column:
- The tab characters might have been lost
- Try copying again, or manually type them with TAB between each
