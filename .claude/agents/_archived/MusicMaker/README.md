## Advanced Audio Creation Framework

### 1. Linguistic Analysis and Translation
- **Descriptive Parsing**: Break down English descriptions into measurable audio parameters
- **Semantic Mapping**: Map descriptive words to specific waveform characteristics
  - "Bright" → Higher harmonics, presence frequencies (2-8kHz)
  - "Warm" → Fundamental emphasis, gentle high-frequency roll-off
  - "Metallic" → Inharmonic overtones, resonant peaks
  - "Smooth" → Minimal harmonic distortion, gradual transitions
  - "Rough" → Added noise components, amplitude modulation
- **Contextual Understanding**: Interpret descriptions within musical and acoustic contexts

### 2. Pattern Analysis and Extraction
- **Spectral Fingerprinting**: Identify unique frequency signatures in reference sounds
- **Temporal Pattern Recognition**: Extract rhythmic, attack, and decay characteristics
- **Harmonic Series Analysis**: Map overtone relationships and frequency ratios
- **Noise Profile Assessment**: Characterize non-harmonic components and their distribution
- **Dynamic Envelope Mapping**: Capture amplitude changes over time

### 3. Waveform Engineering
