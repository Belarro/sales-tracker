## Neuroscientific Knowledge Base

### Brain Architecture
- **Cerebral Cortex**: 6-layered structure with distinct cellular composition
- **Subcortical Structures**: Thalamus, basal ganglia, brainstem, cerebellum
- **White Matter**: Myelinated axon bundles forming connectivity pathways
- **Vascular System**: Blood supply networks critical for neural function
- **Ventricular System**: CSF circulation and brain protection mechanisms

### Cellular Neuroscience
- **Neuron Types**: Pyramidal, interneurons, glial cells with distinct morphologies
- **Synaptic Structure**: Pre/post-synaptic terminals, synaptic cleft, receptor organization
- **Membrane Dynamics**: Ion channels, pumps, and electrical properties
- **Intracellular Organization**: Organelles, cytoskeleton, and protein trafficking

### Neural Dynamics
- **Action Potential Propagation**: Saltatory conduction along myelinated axons
- **Synaptic Transmission**: Chemical and electrical synaptic mechanisms
- **Network Oscillations**: Brain rhythms and synchronized neural activity
- **Plasticity Mechanisms**: Synaptic strengthening, weakening, and structural changes

### Neurotransmitter Systems
- **Excitatory**: Glutamate, acetylcholine pathways and receptor distribution
- **Inhibitory**: GABA, glycine systems and circuit modulation
- **Modulatory**: Dopamine, serotonin, norepinephrine projection systems
- **Neuropeptides**: Specialized signaling molecules and their pathways

## GPU Visualization Architecture

### Rendering Pipeline
1. **Geometry Generation**: 3D mesh creation for brain structures and neurons
2. **Texture Mapping**: Surface properties, connectivity patterns, activity overlays
3. **Shader Programming**: Custom shaders for electrical activity and chemical diffusion
4. **Real-Time Dynamics**: Live simulation of neural activity and signal propagation
5. **Multi-Scale Rendering**: Level-of-detail systems for zoom from molecules to whole brain

### Technical Specifications
- **Vertex Shaders**: Handle 3D transformations and morphological detail
- **Fragment Shaders**: Render electrical activity, neurotransmitter concentrations
- **Compute Shaders**: Simulate neural dynamics and network activity
- **Texture Arrays**: Store connectivity matrices and anatomical atlases
- **Buffer Objects**: Manage large-scale neural network data

### Performance Optimization
- **Level of Detail**: Adaptive rendering based on viewing distance and importance
- **Frustum Culling**: Render only visible neural structures
- **Instanced Rendering**: Efficient rendering of repeated neural elements
- **Temporal Coherence**: Cache stable structures, update only dynamic elements
- **Memory Management**: Optimize GPU memory usage for large-scale neural data
